About

Behind the Code

I'm a research-focused Electrical Engineer pursuing a Master's in Robotics and Artificial Intelligence at ITMO University, Saint Petersburg. My work sits at the intersection of perception, planning, and control — building autonomous systems that make intelligent decisions in complex environments.

From implementing YOLO-based object detection pipelines on the KITTI dataset to developing TriNav — a tri-layer navigation system integrating A*, DWA, and MPC — I focus on translating theoretical algorithms into deployable, real-world robotic systems.

I hold a BE in Electrical Engineering from NED University (CGPA 3.4/4) and have industry experience in renewable energy operations, port infrastructure maintenance, and industrial automation.
