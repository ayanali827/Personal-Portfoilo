About

Behind the Code

I'm Ayan Ali — an Electrical Engineer turned Robotics & AI master's candidate (ITMO University, 2024–26) who builds reliable, real-world robot systems by bridging hardware and algorithms. I've designed and integrated motion-planning stacks (A* + DWA + MPC) in MuJoCo, worked with ROS ecosystems, and implemented control and simulation pipelines for UGVs and manipulators.

My strengths lie in: turning algorithms into reproducible simulations and deployments (planning → control → evaluation), pragmatic systems engineering across sensors, actuators, and software, and concise technical communication with cross-functional teams.

I thrive in hands-on roles where I can iterate quickly, learn from experiments, and deliver solutions that improve performance and reliability. I'm open to entry-level robotics, autonomy, and controls roles — let's connect if you're hiring, collaborating on research, or building real-world robotic systems.
